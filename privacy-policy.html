​Privacy Policy

1. What Info We Grab

We get info in a few ways:

a. Stuff You Give Us

Like your name, email, phone number, and address.

Also, your login info.

Plus, payment details.

Anything you send us through forms or customer support.

b. Stuff We Grab Automatically

Your IP address, device stuff, what kind of computer you’re on, and what browser you use.

How you use our site – what pages you check out, how long you stay, what you click.

Where you are (if you let us see that).

Cookies and other tracking stuff.

c. Info from Other Places

If you log in with Google, Facebook, or Apple.

From partners we work with.

Stuff that's public.

2. How We Put Your Info To Use

We might use your info to:

Keep our services running and make them better.

Process payments.

Make sure you are who you say you are and stop bad guys.

Make your experience more personal.

Send you important notices, security warnings, and deals.

Do some research.

Follow the law.

3. Privacy Stuff Around the World

a. GDPR (Europe &amp; UK)

If you're in Europe or the UK, here's why we use your data:

Because you said it was cool (like for ads).

Because we need it to give you the services you asked for.

Because the law says we have to.

Because it helps us make things better and stop fraud.

You can access, fix, erase, limit, object and move your data.

b. CCPA/CPRA (California)

Californians can:

Ask what info we have and what we do with it.

Ask us to delete your info.

Tell us not to sell your info.

Not be treated unfairly if you use these rights.

We don't sell your info in the normal way, but we might share it with partners for ads, which California might see as selling.

c. Other Places (Brazil, Canada, etc.)

We follow the rules in other countries. You might be able to access, fix, erase, or take back your okay.

4. Who Sees Your Information?

We might share with:

People who process payments, host our site, do analytics, or help with ads.

Partners, to help make things work better together like connecting accounts.

The cops, if they ask.

If we merge with another company.

We don't sell your info for cash.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We hold on to your info only as long as we need it, unless the law says we have to keep it longer. When we don't need it, we delete it or make it anonymous.

6. How We Protect Your Information

We try our best to keep your info safe. But no system is perfect.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

Make the site work.

See how well the site is doing.

Show you ads.

You can turn off cookies in your browser.

8. Kids' Privacy

Our site is not for children under 13. We don't collect their info. If we figure out we do, we'll delete it.

9. Where Your Data Goes

If you're outside [Your Country], your data might go to countries that don't protect privacy the same way.

10. Your Rights

You can:

Ask to see your info

Ask us to fix or delete it

Say no to some ways we use your info

Take back your okay

Say no to getting ads

Complain to the authorities

11. Changes to This Policy

We might change this policy sometimes. If we do, we'll let you know like emailing you.
